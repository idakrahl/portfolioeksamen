Booking/reservations

How can I make a reservation/book a court?

In order to book a court at ProPadel you have to register a profile on our webpage via this link. Registering is free of charge and you will receive your membership ID digits via your assigned e-mail address. Reservations can be made 30 days in advance.

Can I buy a multiple pass/"klippekort"?

No, courts are only available for fixed/regular time slots or single reservations/bookings. 

Can I cancel/reschedule my reservation?

Cancellation/rescheduling of padel courts can be done through your ProPadel-profile under “my bookings". Cancellation/rescheduling must be done no later than 24 hours prior to your initial reservation. 

Will I be reimbursed when cancelling/rescheduling?

The amount will be retransferred to your profile if cancellation/rescheduling takes place no later than 24 hours prior to your initial reservation. The amount can then be spent on another booking at a different time slot. 

Is it possible to get a fixed time slot at one or more of the courts?

Yes, it is possible to purchase regular time slots during a requested time of the week. Please contact info@propadel.dk. for availability and we will try to accommodate your specific needs. 

The courts and instructions on the game padel

Do you have padel courts for single play?

No, all the courts at ProPadel are meant for double’s play. However, it is of course possible to play one-on-one if you wish to do so.

Are your courts indoor?

Yes, all the courts at ProPadel are located indoors with more than 8m to the ceiling.

How do you play padel?

For full instructions on the rules of padel, please refer to this official tutorial videoby Dansk Padel Forbund/Danish Padel Association. The entire set of rules are also available at various places on the boards at the padel centre in Nordhavn. 

Facilities

Can I rent padel bats/rackets at your facilities?

Yes, we have rackets available for rent right next to the entrance to the courts. Payment via Mobile Pay (93429) – 40 DKK/per racket.

Can I change clothes and shower at ProPadel?

Yes, we have changing rooms with shower facilities for both men and women. 

Can I retrieve any forgotten items/belongings?

Yes, any forgotten items/belongings can be picked up at our lost/found box placed next to the vending machine at court number 3. 

Can I buy drinks/refreshments at your facilities?

Yes, we have an assortment of drinks available at the vending machine or at the refrigerators located in the reception or next to the padel courts. Payment with Mobile Pay at the vending machine and by the refrigerators. 

Equipment

Can I buy padel balls at ProPadel?

Yes, padel balls are available for purchase both at the reception (if open) and via the vending machine at the end of court number 3. Payment through Mobile Pay at the reception and at the vending machine. 

Can I buy equipment/gear at the centre?

Yes, we have an assortment of padel bats/rackets, balls, shoes and clothing available for purchase at the reception when open. 

Training & events 

Can I book one or more sessions/classes with a personal trainer?

Yes, we offer individual classes with certified padel trainers. For further information please contact info@propadel.dk. Prices vary between 500-650 DKK depending on trainer, time of day and number of participants.

Do you offer regular classes?

No, but individual/group sessions can be tailor-made according to your needs per request.

Do you offer introductory classes/sessions/events for larger groups of people?

Yes, we do offer introductory sessions for both companies and private groups of people wishing to get started with the sport. The format of the event can be tailor-made to suit your needs. For further information please contact info@propadel.dk.   

Further questions

Please contact us on info@propadel.dk.
